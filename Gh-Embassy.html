Welcome to the website of the Embassy of the Republic of Ghana in the Czech Republic, with concurrent accreditation to the Slovak Republic,Hungary,RomaniaandRepublic of North Macedonia

Welcome to Ghana Embassy Prague It is my honor to welcome you to the website of the Embassy of the Republic of Ghana in the Czech Republic, with concurrent accreditation to the Slovak Republic, Hungary, Republic of Romania and Republic of North Macedonia. I am particularly happy about the fact that you chose to visit this website to acquaint yourself with the services we offer as well as seek relevant information on the latest developments in Ghana. I hope this site would prove useful in your quest for specific information about developments in Ghana. The conducive political environment in Ghana today makes the country one of the best in Africa for not just trade and investment, but for leisure and tourism as well. The creation of this website is therefore intended to furnish the Czech, Hungarian, Macedonian, Romanian and Slovak publics with information on current developments in Ghana, opportunities for mutually beneficial trade and investment, culture, consular services, tourism, among other things. In our attempt to achieve the above, your valuable contributions and suggestions are very welcome to enhancing the Embassy’s realization of the objectives of establishing this website as well as improving our services. We’re happy to hear from you… Please do not hesitate to forward your questions and comments to prague@mfa.gov.gh
